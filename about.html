Our Story

Bringing beauty and joy through flowers since 2010

How It All Began

Blooming Petals started as a small family-owned flower shop in 2010. What began as a passion project quickly blossomed into something much bigger. Our founder, Sarah Johnson, inherited her love for flowers from her grandmother, who taught her the art of flower arrangement.

Over the years, we've grown from a single shop to multiple locations across the city, but our commitment to quality and personal service remains unchanged. We take pride in sourcing the freshest flowers and creating stunning arrangements that bring joy to our customers.

Today, we continue to grow and innovate while staying true to our roots - delivering happiness through beautiful flowers.
